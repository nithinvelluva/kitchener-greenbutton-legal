Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using the Kitchener Green Button Demo App (“the Service”).

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth tokens granted by your utility provider via the Green Button API.
  • Utility usage and billing data retrieved from your provider’s API.
  • Minimal technical logs for troubleshooting (no sensitive personal data).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to display your authorized utility information within the Service. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required by law.

3. Data Storage

Tokens and data are stored temporarily in memory for demonstration purposes. In production, data should be stored securely in a database with encryption at rest and in transit.

4. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You may revoke the Service’s access to your utility data at any time via your utility provider’s account portal.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here.
